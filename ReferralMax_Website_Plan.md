## 1. Strategic Brand Foundation

### 1.1 Positioning Statement
ReferralMax is the referral and affiliate engine built for *Main Street*, not just Silicon Valley. We turn happy customers, loyal fans, and partner businesses into a measurable growth channel — and we pay them back in real, spendable rewards through instant digital credit cards. For small and mid-sized businesses, ReferralMax is the simplest way to launch a word-of-mouth program that actually drives revenue, without the spreadsheets, the PayPal headaches, or the "we'll mail you a gift card in 6–8 weeks."

### 1.2 Elevator Pitch (one paragraph)
ReferralMax helps small businesses grow the way they've always grown best — through the people who already love them. Businesses create a branded referral program in minutes, hand out unique codes to customers, employees, and affiliate partners, and let ReferralMax handle the rest. When a code is redeemed, the referred customer gets an instant discount, and the person who shared it gets a real digital Visa-style credit card dropped straight into their wallet. It's the most honest, human, and frictionless way to reward the people who help your business grow.

### 1.3 Tagline System
- Primary (from existing logo): **Refer. Earn. Succeed.**
- Hero alternates to rotate / A/B test:
  - *Turn every customer into a growth channel.*
  - *Word-of-mouth, finally measurable.*
  - *Your best salespeople already love you. Pay them.*
  - *Referrals in. Revenue up. Rewards out.*

### 1.4 Voice & Tone
Confident, plainspoken, and small-business-friendly. We sound like the smartest person at the Chamber of Commerce mixer — not a VC-funded growth-hacker. We avoid jargon ("attribution waterfalls," "viral loops"), we use concrete numbers, and we always lead with what the *owner* gets, then what the *advocate* gets, then what the *new customer* gets.

### 1.5 Brand Color System (from the logo set)
- **Primary Blue** `#1E5BA8` — trust, stability, financial credibility
- **Growth Green** `#2EB84C` — earnings, success, go-signals
- **Accent Orange** `#F58220` — action, warmth, CTAs
- **Deep Navy** `#0E2A47` — headings, premium surfaces
- **Soft White** `#F7F9FC` — page background
- **Slate** `#4A5568` — body copy

### 1.6 Target Audiences (and what each cares about)
1. **Small & mid-sized business owners** (restaurants, med-spas, salons, home services, boutique retail, e-commerce shops, B2B service firms, insurance agents, gyms, dentists). They care about: predictable new-customer acquisition, low CAC, no tech lift, "set it and forget it."
2. **Existing customers / brand advocates.** They care about: easy sharing, instant gratification, real money (not points), feeling like a VIP.
3. **Affiliate partners & micro-influencers.** They care about: transparent tracking, fast payouts, professional dashboards, no minimum thresholds.
4. **Referred new customers.** They care about: a meaningful discount, a trustworthy brand, a fast checkout.

## 2. Competitive Landscape & How We Win

We study and borrow the best of **ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Referral Rock, PartnerStack, Post Affiliate Pro, Tapfiliate,** and **Refersion** — then we do the one thing none of them do well: **pay advocates in real, instant, spendable dollars via digital credit cards, the moment a referral converts.**

Our defensible wedge:
- **Instant digital credit card rewards** (not points, not store credit, not a manual check in the mail).
- **Built for small business**, not enterprise SaaS buyers.
- **Dual incentive out of the box** — discount for the new customer *and* cash-card for the advocate.
- **No-code launch** in under 15 minutes.
- **Flat, predictable pricing** (no per-transaction gouging).

## 5. Technical & Design Direction

- **Stack:** Next.js 14 (App Router) + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui + Framer Motion for micro-interactions.
- **CMS:** Content as MDX in-repo for v1; migrate to Sanity or Contentlayer later.
- **Forms:** Lead form → HubSpot or Resend-to-email; trial signup → placeholder POST to `/api/waitlist` for v1.
- **Analytics:** Plausible or PostHog, plus Google Analytics 4.
- **Hosting:** Vercel, connected to the GitHub repo. CI on every PR.
- **Performance targets:** Lighthouse 95+ across the board; LCP under 1.8s on 4G; fully accessible (WCAG 2.2 AA).
- **SEO:** Per-page metadata, Open Graph images, sitemap, robots.txt, JSON-LD for Organization and Product.
- **Imagery style:** Bright, real-human photography of small-business operators (not stock-photo suits). Vector illustrations in brand blue/green/orange for the "how it works" and "digital card" sections.
- **Motion language:** Subtle. Numbers count up on scroll. Cards lift 2px on hover. Nothing that delays interaction.
