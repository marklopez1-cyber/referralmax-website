How It Works

Launch a real referral program in three simple steps.

No developers. No spreadsheets. No checks in the mail. Just word-of-mouth growth, automated end-to-end.

1

Set up your program in minutes

Pick your reward amount, your customer discount, and your branding. We generate unique codes and shareable links for every customer, employee, and affiliate partner.

2

Your advocates share. We track everything.

Customers share by text, email, social, or QR code. ReferralMax attributes every click, sign-up, and sale to the right person — in real time.

3

New customers save. Advocates get paid instantly.

Referred customers get an automatic discount at checkout. Their advocate gets a digital credit card loaded with the reward — ready to spend in seconds.
